The purpose of this project was to create an easily accessible resource for landowners and the general public in regards to the emerald ash borer invasion, to be published on the Virginia Department of Forestryâ€™s webpage. This information includes signs that an EAB infestation has begun, as well as methods of managing individual ash trees or populations to both prevent, monitor, and reduce the impact of infestations. While information on management is highly important, I also wanted to emphasize what individuals can do to slow or prevent the future spread of these insects.
