About Me

Hello! My name is Kendra Counts. I come from a little town called Grottoes, Virginia and am currently living nearby in Harrisonburg. I graduated from the University of Virginia in 2017 with a B.A. in Environmental Sciences.

I'm currently honing my skillsets while prepping for a cross-country move to New Mexico, where I hope to find environmental- or GIS-based work. My future plans include further education in one or both of these fields, with an emphasis on the latter.

I enjoy painting (especially with watercolors), building model aircraft kits, baking, and thinking about game design. I believe that the best time to walk through the woods is in the rain.
