Land Cover Maps

These maps were created to be included in land management plans for Virginia Forestry and Wildlife Group clients. Therefore, they needed to accurately and clearly convey information to clients, while being a useful reference point for the company. I emphasized aesthetics and readability as a result.

These began with manual land cover surveys conducted by the company. I then hand digitized the drawn maps in ArcMap and calculated the acreage for each land cover type present. Digitization included tracking down county parcel boundary information. I created a set of terrain, topographic, and small-scale location maps for each project.
